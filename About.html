About me

I am an italian graduate physics student. My studies were focused on pacific applications of nuclear physics, like radiation protection, imaging and radiotherapy.  I am enrolled as a Ph.D student in Neutronics at the Danmarks Tekniske Universitet. Currently working in the HighNESS project on the design of the beam extraction system for the high intensity cold neutron source at the ESS. Genuinely loving mountain trekking, nature and slow travels. Also tech curious and supporter of crypto revolution.

What makes scientists happy? 
Probably collect good quality data, pushing the instruments to their limits and fit that data to physical models.
